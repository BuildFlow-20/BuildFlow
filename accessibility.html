Accessibility

Inclusive design is part of every website we build.

BuildFlow creates accessible websites that are easier to use for everyone, including people with disabilities, low vision, and mobile users.

Our Accessibility Principles

Semantic HTML

We structure content with proper headings, landmarks, and accessible elements.

Keyboard Navigation

Pages are usable without a mouse, with clear focus states and logical tab order.

Readable Contrast

We use strong color contrast for text, buttons, and interactive elements.

Alt Text & Media

Images include descriptive alt text and accessible media labels where needed.

What We Deliver
  • Accessible forms with labels and clear instructions
  • Logical heading structure for easier screen reader navigation
  • Responsive design that works across devices and assistive tools
  • Performance optimizations that reduce barriers for slower connections
Why It Matters

Accessibility improves usability for everyone, increases reach, and supports a better brand experience. It is a practical and ethical part of modern web design.
