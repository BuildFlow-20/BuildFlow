Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our process, pricing, timelines, and support for digital projects.

How long does it take to build a website?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity. Most small business websites launch within 2–4 weeks, while larger custom builds may take 6–12 weeks.

What services does BuildFlow offer?

We deliver website design and development, mobile apps, AI solutions, e-commerce stores, branding support, and ongoing maintenance.

How much does a project typically cost?

BuildFlow pricing depends on scope. Small websites often start around $499, while custom applications and AI projects are priced based on features and integrations.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes, we include revision rounds as part of project delivery. The exact number of revisions is outlined in each proposal.

What information do you need to get started?

We typically need your business goals, branding assets, preferred style examples, target audience, and any functional requirements.

How can I contact BuildFlow?

Send us a message through our contact page or email us directly at hello@buildflow.com.
