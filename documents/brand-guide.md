# Brand Guide

## Brand Overview

BuildFlow is a modern web design and development studio focused on fast, responsive, and business-ready websites.

## Logo

- Use the BuildFlow wordmark clearly and consistently.
- Ensure there is sufficient spacing around the logo.

## Colors

- Primary: #2563eb
- Secondary: #22c55e
- Accent: #93c5fd
- Background: #0f172a
- Text: #f8fafc

## Typography

- Headings: bold, modern sans-serif
- Body: clean, readable sans-serif

## Voice & Tone

- Clear and professional
- Confident and helpful
- Focused on business growth and quality delivery

## Usage

- Use simple, friendly language.
- Highlight benefits clearly.
- Keep layouts clean, fast, and responsive.
